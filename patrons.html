Patrons

We would like to introduce you to our extremely talented advisors who always motivate us for further innovations- Our Patrons

Dr. C. Muthamizhchelvan Sir

 

Dr. C. Muthamizhchelvan has been part of SRM group since inception and has served various academic and administrative roles for the last 30 years. With 21 years of academic contribution and 9 years of Research guidance and Administrative contributions currently holds the position of Director of Faculty of Engineering and Technology. He has obtained his Ph.D. in Anna University, Chennai and his Masters from IIT Chennai. His research alignment is broadly in the areas of Nanoscience and Nano technology. He has guided 7 research scholars in fulfillment of their Ph.D. degree and presently administers 5 full time research scholars. He has over 90 International journal publications and has presented 28 papers in International conferences. Shizuoka University, Japan has recognized him as Honorable Guest Professor from April 2014.
